Our commitment to protecting your data and your vote

Introduction

Your privacy is a top priority for TRUECAST. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in our secure, blockchain-based digital voting system. By using our service, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

To ensure secure and verifiable voting, we collect certain information during the registration and authentication process.

Registration Data

  • Personal Information: Name, address, date of birth, and contact details (email, phone number). This information is used for identity verification and is stored securely.
  • ID Documents: We process images or scans of official identification documents to verify voter eligibility. These documents are securely deleted after verification is complete.
  • Biometric Data: Facial recognition and/or fingerprint data is converted into a cryptographic template. This template is used for authentication and cannot be reverse-engineered to reconstruct your biometric features. It is stored separately from other personal data.

Voting Data

  • Vote Selections: Your vote is encrypted and stored on an immutable blockchain ledger. It is associated with a cryptographic hash, not your personal identity, ensuring your vote is anonymous.
  • Transaction Data: The blockchain records transaction details such as the transaction hash, block number, and timestamp. This data is public for transparency and auditability.

Location Data

  • Geo-Verification: For certain elections, we may use your device's location to verify that you are within an authorized voting region. This location data is used only at the time of verification and is not stored.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and confirm your eligibility to vote.
  • To provide multi-factor and biometric authentication for secure access.
  • To record and secure your vote on the blockchain.
  • To ensure the integrity and prevent fraud in the voting process.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

Data Security

TRUECAST employs advanced security measures to protect your data.

  • Blockchain Technology: Your vote is stored on an immutable, decentralized ledger, which makes it impossible to alter or delete.
  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is protected by end-to-end encryption.
  • Anonymity: Your vote is decoupled from your personal identity through cryptographic techniques.
  • Access Control: Access to personal information is strictly limited to authorized personnel with a need-to-know basis.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data (subject to legal obligations and the immutable nature of the blockchain).
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing (where applicable).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
