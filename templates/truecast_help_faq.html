General Questions

What is TRUECAST? +
TRUECAST is a secure, blockchain-based digital voting system designed to make elections more transparent and accessible. It uses multi-factor authentication and cryptographic verification to ensure the integrity of every vote.
Is my vote anonymous? +
Yes. While every vote is recorded on the blockchain for transparency, your personal identity is securely encrypted and not linked to your vote. This ensures your privacy and anonymity.
What are the system requirements to use TRUECAST? +
TRUECAST is designed to be accessible on most modern devices. You will need a stable internet connection and a device with a modern web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari). Biometric features (face/fingerprint) require a compatible device.

Registration & Login

How do I register to vote? +
You can register by visiting the Voter Registration page. The process involves four steps: providing personal information, verifying your identity with official documents, setting up biometric authentication, and accepting the terms of service.
I forgot my login credentials. What should I do? +
Your login is based on your unique Voter ID and your chosen authentication method (biometric, OTP, or backup PIN). If you have trouble, you can use the SMS/Email OTP method to regain access.
My biometric scan isn't working. +
Ensure you are in a well-lit area for facial recognition. For fingerprint scans, make sure your finger and the sensor are clean. If the issue persists, you can always use your backup PIN or OTP to log in.

Voting Process

How do I cast my vote? +
After logging in, you will be taken to the voting dashboard. Simply click on your preferred candidate or option for each race. Once all selections are made, click the "Cast Vote" button to submit your ballot.
Can I change my vote after it's cast? +
No. Due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology, once a vote is cast and recorded, it cannot be changed or deleted. Please review your selections carefully before submitting your ballot.
How can I be sure my vote was counted? +
After casting your vote, you will receive a unique **transaction hash**. You can use this hash on the Vote Verification page to confirm that your vote was successfully recorded on the blockchain.
