Voter Registration

‚ö†Ô∏è
You must be a registered voter in your jurisdiction to use TRUECAST. The registration process includes identity verification and security setup.

Registration Requirements

To register for TRUECAST voting, you'll need:

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, lease agreement, etc.)
  • Email address and phone number
  • Device with camera for biometric setup

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does registration take? ‚ñº

Registration typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete. Identity verification may take up to 24 hours to process.

What if I don't have a smartphone? ‚ñº

You can use any device with a camera (computer, tablet) for biometric setup. Alternative authentication methods like PIN are available for voting.

Can I register on election day? ‚ñº

Registration must be completed at least 48 hours before election day to allow for identity verification and security checks.

Vote Verification

üîç
TRUECAST provides transparent vote verification. You can confirm your vote was recorded correctly without compromising ballot secrecy.

How to Verify Your Vote

After casting your vote, you'll receive a unique transaction hash. Use this hash to:

  • Confirm your vote was recorded on the blockchain
  • Verify the timestamp and block information
  • Check that your vote hasn't been tampered with
  • Download an official verification receipt

Understanding Verification Results

What does "blockchain confirmed" mean? ‚ñº

This means your vote has been permanently recorded on the blockchain and has received the required number of confirmations from network validators.

Can I see who I voted for in verification? ‚ñº

No, the verification system only confirms that your vote was recorded. The actual vote choices remain encrypted and anonymous to protect ballot secrecy.

What if verification fails? ‚ñº

If verification fails, contact election officials immediately. You may need to cast a provisional ballot or re-vote if within the allowed timeframe.

Security & Privacy

üõ°Ô∏è
TRUECAST uses military-grade encryption, blockchain technology, and multiple layers of security to protect your vote and privacy.

Security Features

  • End-to-End Encryption: Your vote is encrypted from the moment you cast it
  • Blockchain Storage: Votes are stored on an immutable, distributed ledger
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: Biometric, OTP, and PIN verification
  • Zero-Knowledge Proofs: Verify votes without revealing vote content
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
  • Audit Trails: Complete transaction history for transparency

Privacy Protection

We protect your privacy through:

  • Anonymous vote casting (voter identity separate from vote content)
  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • No tracking of individual vote choices
  • Minimal data collection (only what's required for voting)
  • Automatic data deletion after retention period

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

I can't log in to my account ‚ñº

Possible solutions:

  • Check that you're entering the correct Voter ID
  • Ensure your authentication method (biometric/OTP/PIN) is working
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Contact support if the issue persists
Biometric authentication isn't working ‚ñº

Try these steps:

  • Ensure your camera/sensor is clean and unobstructed
  • Verify camera permissions are enabled for TRUECAST
  • Try in better lighting conditions
  • Use your backup PIN instead
  • Re-register your biometric data if needed
The website is loading slowly or not at all ‚ñº

Network troubleshooting:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Switch to a different network if available
  • Contact your IT support if on an organizational network
I didn't receive my OTP code ‚ñº

OTP delivery issues:

  • Check your spam/junk folder for email OTP
  • Verify your phone number is correct for SMS OTP
  • Wait up to 5 minutes for delivery
  • Try requesting a new code
  • Use alternative authentication method (PIN or biometric)
My vote verification shows an error ‚ñº

Verification issues:

  • Double-check you entered the transaction hash correctly
  • Wait a few minutes for blockchain confirmation
  • Try the verification again later
  • Contact election officials immediately if error persists
  • Keep your transaction hash receipt for reference
